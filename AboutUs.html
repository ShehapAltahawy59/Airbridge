How to Trust Us

The sender is fully responsible for paying the total cost of the item and delivery fee, ensuring they are aware of the product's value. The amount will be held in the system until the state of the request changes to "Delivered" or "Cancelled." This ensures fair handling of disputes while maintaining transparency and accountability between senders, travelers, and the platform. The system administrator oversees payment suspensions, ensuring secure transactions and timely updates.
